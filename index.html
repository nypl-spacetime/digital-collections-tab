NYPL Digital Collections Tab

View a historic image from NYPL Digital Collections, every time you open a new tab.

Help put New York City’s history on the map!

Know your NYC geography? Click the link at the bottom of the tab to view the image in The New York Public Library’s Surveyor tool. Geotag the image and help put New York City’s history on the map!

Acknowledgements

NYPL Digital Collections Tab is part of the NYC Space/Time Directory. The goal of this project is to—through a variety of resources—unlock the potential of historical maps and provide opportunities to explore urban history across space and time.

Major support for the NYC Space/Time Directory is provided by the Knight News Challenge, an initiative of the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation.

Uninstalling

Google Chrome: to remove this extension from your browser, go to Chrome’s Extensions settings page.

Other browsers: see GitHub.
